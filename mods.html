Mods / News

This page lists a bunch of Mods of and News about New Super Mario Bros. 2 !

[DEMO Released] Super Mario Bros 3. REVAMPED!

This mod develpped by HD Erick Games is a fan recreation of Super Mario Bros 3 inside of NSMB2 !

It is currently in a DEMO state, which you can download at Kuribo64 !

Super Mario Bros. Next

Super Mario Bros. Next is an upcoming mod made by the SMBNext Team that features brand new enemies and mechanics !

Find out more on their Official Website !

SMBNext: Halloween Special + DX

Two mods made by the SMBNext Team that are theamed around Halloween !

BooOOooOo...

Those mods are available on their Official Website !

Sunset Shores

Made by the SMBNext Team, this mod is a short and delightful adventure theamed around sunny beaches and sand shores !

Find out more on their Official Website !

More Super Mario Bros. 3DS

Made by UptownRanger, is an upcoming simple level and tileset rom hack of NSMB2 !

You can download the demo for this mod at Kuribo64 !

NSMB2: Mastermode!!

This mod by NZia remixes the original stages with an accent on difficulty for those who wants a new challenge !

You can download the demo for this mod at Kuribo64 !

[Demo] New Super Mario Bros. 2 Anew! v.1.2

A first an ambitious hack of GreyMask that will revamp all 9 Worlds Levels !

Find out more at Kuribo64 !

New Super Mario Bros. 2 Take 2

New Super Mario Bros. 2 Take 2, made by Thorge, will be a full 93 Level hack with currently a 4 levels demo released !

Check it out on the Official Website !

New Super Mario Redux

Made by TRS, this hack is sort-of a port of one of his NSMB hack, meant to be fresh and new !

A World 1 Demo is available at Kuribo64 !
